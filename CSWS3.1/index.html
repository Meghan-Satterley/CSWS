Who We Are

Consolidated Social Work Services, Inc (CSWS) is a grass-roots, non-profit organization that works to connect people to resources and advocate for the needs of our community. CSWS provides direct and referral services to those in need while assisting individuals and communities in obtaining social services. We network and collaborate with other individuals, groups and organizations to enhance community safety and wellness.

Our Mission

MISSION

Mission

CSWS mission is to empower the individual and aid in their journey by providing wrap around support programs and services to the community

VISION

Vision

CSWS believes in the importance of community and strives to create a neighborhood that provides the opportunity for each person to achieve their fullest potential.

VALUES

Values

CSWS believes in the importance of justice, dignity, fairness, caring, equality, self-esteem, and self-sufficiency.

Local Projects

School Supplies Drive

Martin Luther King Jr. Elementary is a local school of 600 students who is struggling to accommodate their students' need for supplies. The CSWS volunteer Neighbors Group worked to collect supplies through supporter donations, as well as donations graciously received from other supporters of the community. Through the work of our amazing community, we were about to deliver ten boxes of school supplies!

The schools that work to serve our community are in so much need and CSWS takes pride and joy in sharing some supplies to ease the burden.

Distributing Hygiene Bags

CSWS strives to serve our unhoused community supporters with any resources they may be lacking. Our Neighbors Group met to put together hygiene bags containing: socks, towels, shampoo, a toothbrush, toothpaste, hand warmers, razors, feminine hygiene items, soap, and other important necessities.

We value all supporters of our community and take pride in sharing resources when we can!
